Jamus is a conceptually driven, tech-savvy, multidisciplinary graphic and motion designer, creative thinker, and problem solver interested in creating original, unexpected, and intelligent design that communicates authentic ideas/stories, and creates purposeful and meaningful experiences that advance goals, touch hearts, provoke the use of heads, and charm eyes.

Jamus holds a BFA in graphic design from the School of Visual Arts, is a recipient of the Silas H. Rhodes Scholarship, and was awarded The Rhodes Family Award for Outstanding Achievement in Graphic Design as well as the Boyzzz Scholarship Award for Motion Graphics.

His talents have been developed at SpotCo under the art direction of Gail Anderson, working at RED Partners on a wide range of projects including branding, book design, book covers, posters, environmental graphics, event graphics, and motion graphics for various clients, creating award winning theater posters with Kevin Brainard of Area of Practice, and as a freelance designer and consultant while living abroad developing and broadening his character and cultural perspective volunteering.

Jamus is available for freelance work, collaborations, and full-time hire.
